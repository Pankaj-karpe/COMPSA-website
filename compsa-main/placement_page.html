Placement Process

Your journey from student to professional

1

Registration & Profile Building

Students register with the placement cell and create comprehensive profiles highlighting their skills, projects, and achievements. Resume reviews and optimization are provided.

2

Pre-Placement Training

Intensive training programs covering aptitude, technical skills, communication, group discussions, and personal interview preparation. Mock tests and practice sessions conducted regularly.

3

Company Pre-Placement Talk

Invited companies present their organization, job roles, expectations, and selection criteria. Students get opportunity to interact and clarify doubts about the company.

4

Written Test / Online Assessment

Companies conduct aptitude tests, technical assessments, and coding challenges. Students are shortlisted based on performance for further rounds.

5

Group Discussion (if applicable)

Shortlisted candidates participate in group discussions to assess communication skills, leadership qualities, and ability to work in teams.

6

Technical & HR Interviews

Multiple rounds of technical interviews assess problem-solving skills and domain knowledge. HR interviews evaluate cultural fit and career aspirations.

7

Final Selection & Offer

Selected candidates receive offer letters with detailed compensation and joining information. Placement cell facilitates the entire process ensuring smooth communication.

Training Programs

Preparing you for success

Aptitude Training

Comprehensive training in quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, verbal ability, and data interpretation. Regular tests to track progress and improvement.

Technical Training

In-depth sessions on data structures, algorithms, system design, database management, and programming languages. Hands-on coding practice and problem-solving.

Soft Skills Development

Communication skills, presentation techniques, email etiquette, and professional behavior training. Building confidence for interviews and workplace success.

Group Discussion Training

Practice sessions on various topics, learning to articulate thoughts clearly, listen actively, and lead discussions effectively in team settings.

Mock Interviews

Simulated interview experiences with industry professionals and faculty. Detailed feedback on technical knowledge, communication, and body language.

Resume Building

Workshops on creating impactful resumes and cover letters. One-on-one sessions to optimize your profile and highlight achievements effectively.
